Customer Service

Returns & Exchanges

J.R. Watkins Refund Policy

To obtain a refund for the J.R. Watkins items you purchased at JRWatkins.com, please send an e-mail to webhelp@jrwatkins.com or fill in the Contact Us form with your request for a refund or an exchange within 30 days of your original purchase. Or contact us toll free at 1-800-243-9423 Monday-Friday 8 am-5 pm Central Time.

Unfortunately, returns cannot be authorized for products purchased from other online vendors. We are also unable to accept returns or offer refunds for limited edition, discontinued or sale items.

Damaged Packages

When your order arrives, please inspect the package for any damage that may have occurred during shipment. If damage occurred to the item(s) in your shipment, please contact Watkins Customer Service immediately at 1-800-243-9423 Monday-Friday 8 am-5 pm, CT or email webhelp@jrwatkins.com. You may also simply fill in the Contact Us form.

Shipping Policy

Shipping Rates

Overview

In the U.S., please allow up to two business days to process your online order and approximately five to seven business days for shipping. Orders shipping to Canada, please allow up to two business days to process your order and approximately 2-9 business days for shipping.

Primary shipping carrier is UPS in the US. UPS SurePost may be used upon the discretion of JR Watkins. Purolator and Canada Post for Canada.

Expedited Shipping

At this time, overnight service is only available in the US through our toll-free Order Department. For rushed delivery, please call 1-800-Watkins (928-5467), Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., CT. An operator will be more than happy to assist you. An additional charge will apply.

Shipping Regulations With Hazardous Products

Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, Virgin Islands, and APO/FPO Addresses.

Hazardous products cannot be shipped to these remote locations in Canada.

Due to shipping regulations with hazardous products, we are no longer able to ship those products and assortments. View the list of Hazardous Products.

We do not ship products internationally (outside of the U.S. and Canada).
